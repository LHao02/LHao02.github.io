abstract: >-
  This paper introduces MS-LGNet, a novel method that integrates multi-scale local temporal modeling with global temporal dynamic modeling and uses domain-adversarial training for cross-domain alignment to improve inertial gait-based pedestrian recognition under covariate (disguise) perturbations. On the DIGRD disguised inertial gait recognition dataset, MS-LGNet increases the classification accuracy by 5.83% and 5.98% under the clothing and shoe-type covariates, respectively.
