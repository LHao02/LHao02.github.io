    <h2>Boxing Corner</h2>

    <p>
    Sanda training has been an important part of my life outside academic research. It is not only a form of physical exercise, but also a way for me to cultivate discipline, resilience, focus, and self-control.

    During training, every punch, kick, and movement requires repeated practice and careful adjustment. This process is similar to scientific research: progress does not come from a single breakthrough, but from continuous effort, reflection, and refinement. Through Sanda, I have learned how to stay calm under pressure, face challenges directly, and keep improving through persistence.
    <p>
    For me, Sanda represents a balance between strength and strategy. It reminds me that a strong mindset is as important as technical ability, whether in sports, study, or research. These training experiences have helped me build confidence and perseverance, which also support my long-term growth as an engineering student and researcher.
